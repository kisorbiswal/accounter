Accounts Receivable


The Accounts Receivable (A/R) module maintains tight controls over cash, receivables and customer analysis, billing, cash receipts and collections processes.


Most importantly, Accounts Receivable capitalizes on the ease of use, staff productivity, return on investment and business intelligence.


Advantages of Accounts Receivable module:


Generate invoices, accelerate collections, reduce credit risk and resolve exceptions quickly


Faster periodic cycles of operations


Use work flow automation to reduce the invoice generation and distribution process


Help increase customer service levels and satisfaction with on-demand sales, receivable and payment information and complete customer history
